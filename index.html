SHERAH OUTREACH INITIATIVE

Building Cities one life at a time. 1 Chronicles 7:24

A Legacy project: Invest in the future! Pay a child’s school fees!

About 20 years ago I came across a verse in the Bible that struck a chord deep inside me. I began to see myself as Sherah – a builder of cities (schools) since I have been an educator for more than 40 years. However, as always happens when the Lord wants you to reflect deeper, I adjusted that vision to the one of building cities one life at a time.

In the very early days of my walk with the Lord I had the awesome privilege of access to an old collection of Christian books – autobiographies and biographies of men and women who had given up all to follow the Lord and obey His Word – Matt. 28: 18 – 20

When I read about their exploits, I believed this was the type of life every believer has been called to live. So, when I got an opportunity to meet a present-day missionary (who had truly given up all) and get involved in her mission field I could not hesitate. With time I began to support a few more missionaries on a monthly basis.

In March 2021, the Lord laid it upon my heart to take a step of faith in regard to a higher level of commitment to missionaries: to get involved in the provision of good quality of education for their children even if it involves putting them in boarding schools. This gave birth to the Sherah Outreach Initiative (RC 163964), an NGO registered by the Federal Government of Nigeria.

The education of their children has always been a great burden for missionaries especially those in rural areas. Some have even lost children to human traffickers due to false promises of help.

SOI undertakes to educate children of missionaries in the best schools nearest to them; this frees the missionaries to undertake their assignments with joy and gives the children a practical demonstration of God’s love and provision. All the children in each family have their fees fully paid.
